About Red Velvet

Red Velvet (레드벨벳) is a 5-member South Korean girl group under SM Entertainment, consisting of Irene, Seulgi, Wendy, Joy, and Yeri.

They debuted on August 1, 2014 with their first single, 행복 (Happiness). On April 4, 2025 it was announced that Wendy and Yeri's contracts with SM Ent. have ended. Despite the contract changes, both Wendy and Yeri will continue as active members of Red Velvet and collaborate with SM Entertainment for group-related projects.

The group's name represents its concept, defined by two different images, "Red" and "Velvet". "Red" signifies their vivid and bold image, whereas "Velvet" reflects their classy and toned-down side.

Official Color

Red Velvet's official color is pastel coral, and it's used to represent the group's unique and vibrant aesthetic. The color is chosen for its cute and soft feel, which aligns with the group's "velvet" side, where they present a more elegant and mature persona.

Fandom Name

The fandom name "ReVeluv" for Red Velvet combines "rêve," meaning "dream" in French, with "luv," a shortened version of "love". The name signifies that fans help Red Velvet's dreams come true, and Red Velvet also affectionately calls its fans "luvies".
